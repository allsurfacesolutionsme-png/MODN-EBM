# Collision Definition

In MODN-EBM, collision is not an explicit interaction rule.

Instead, it is derived from field structure.

---

## Definition

C(i,j,t) = |∇Φ(i,j,t)|²

---

## Interpretation

- Low C → smooth, stable region
- High C → interaction or transition region

---

## Meaning

Collision represents:
- gradient intensity
- structural change zones
- emergent interaction points

---

## Key Insight

Collisions are not events added to the system.

They are a direct consequence of spatial variation in Φ.
